Jill Pickell

Rock & Bowl rocked my world! I, Jill Pickell, am a professional food critic and I can say without a doubt that Rock & Bowl is in the top 5 food trucks I have ever gone to! Their crispy bread perfectly complements the chicken soup. The bread doesn’t get soggy, it instead adds it’s crisp on top of the soup flavor! For a meal this good, I would easily pay over twelve dollars, but Rock & Bowl expects only three fourths of that! Not to mention they donate extra food to homeless shelters! They serve delicious food and are generous too! Even if you aren’t a fan of rock, this place still has awesome soup.

Soro

I expected a simple snack when I was running late to work, and got so much more at Rock & Bowl! My meal was a crispy and hearty bread bowl filled with a hot, homestyle, chicken soup. Best of all I could hold the bowl in my hand easily while I was on the run. It was like a transportable, yet delectable hand warmer. The food truck was radiant, and the chef’s and employees smiles, even more! The rock music was a blast to the past for me and my favorite artist was rockin’ on an electric guitar. In New York Review, it's hard to find a restaurant that welcomes me with open arms, but the camaraderie here was tangible. They even had a cute guitar mascot! I could tell the food was freshly made and had premium quality. Tasted exactly like my Grandma’s. The service was so quick, that I was early to my office. The mouthwatering smell of that chicken soup trailed me all day, I definitely recommend!
